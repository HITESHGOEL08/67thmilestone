Campus Ambassador Program

"It is your turn to be the evangelist of your college and bring out a marketing ninja!"
Introduction

Campus Ambassador is responsible for the publicity and marketing of 67th Milestone’18 and is responsible for introducing, briefing and encouraging student participation in the fest, in his/her college/university. He/She is the chief student representative of their college and will be the coordinator for any relevant activity conducted in their respective college for publicity through social media, college notice boards, root-mails etc.

Details
  • The program will last until the end of 67th Milestone’18.
  • Deadline for Applications: March 25, 2018.
  • Eligibility Criteria:- Must be a college student (of any year)
  • Responsibility of CA:- To create awareness about 67th Milestone'18 through social media marketing, word of mouth, offline marketing, and bringing maximum participation from his/her college/university.
  • The partners will be rewarded with exciting prizes and recommendations.
  • Only active partners who meet the required target will be considered for any kind of reward.
Benefits
  • Certificate of Appreciation
  • Letter of Recommendation
  • Exemption from Registration Fee and Star Night Passes. (for Top Performers)
  • Learn Marketing Skills
  • Enhance your convincing power
  • Best Campus Partner Award
  • Exciting Prizes and goodies (for Top Performers)
  • Exclusive Merchandise (67th Milestone T-Shirts)
